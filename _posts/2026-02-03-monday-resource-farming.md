Monday is all about setting yourself up for success for the rest of the week. Here's how to maximize your resource farming:

## Priority Tasks

1. **Complete Daily Missions Early**
   - Don't wait until the last minute
   - Maximize your daily reward bonuses

2. **Focus on Resource Nodes**
   - Hit all resource nodes on the map
   - Prioritize higher-level nodes for better rewards

3. **Alliance Donations**
   - Get your alliance contributions in early
   - Coordinate with teammates for bonus rewards

## Pro Tips

- Save your energy refills for Tuesday's event (see Tuesday tips)
- Stock up on basic materials - you'll need them for Wednesday crafting
- Don't forget to collect your alliance mailbox rewards

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Don't spend all your resources on Monday - save some for mid-week events
- Remember to upgrade your resource collectors before logging off
